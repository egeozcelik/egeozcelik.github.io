Privacy Policy

Moneyflow+ respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information that can identify you directly.

1. Data Used by Third Parties

Moneyflow+ displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use certain non-personal identifiers (such as a device ID or general interaction data) to show non-personalized ads. These identifiers do not reveal your identity and are not used for tracking across other apps or websites.

2. Data We Collect

Moneyflow+ itself does not collect, store, or transmit any user data, analytics, or financial information you enter. All data entered by you stays locally on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

Advertising in Moneyflow+ is handled by Google AdMob. Learn more about Google’s data practices here:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Your Choices

You can limit ad personalization in your device settings:
• iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking
• Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
