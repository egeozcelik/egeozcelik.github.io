Hangman - Challenge

Privacy Policy

Hangman - Challenge is a simple and entertaining word guessing game designed for users of all ages. Players try to guess words letter by letter while attempting to beat their personal high scores. The game operates entirely offline with local data storage, ensuring a private and secure gaming experience without requiring any personal information or user authentication.

This Privacy Policy describes how Hangman - Challenge ("we", "us", "our" or "Hangman - Challenge") collects, uses and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application ("Application"). By using Hangman - Challenge, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide: Hangman - Challenge does not collect, request, or store any personal information, including but not limited to your first name, last name, phone number, email address, or any other personal details. The app operates without any user registration or authentication system, allowing you to download and play immediately.

Game Data: Your high scores stored locally on your device. This information remains entirely on your device and is not transmitted to external servers or shared with third parties.

No User Authentication: The application does not require any form of user registration, login, or authentication. You can start playing immediately after downloading the app.

2. Use of Your Information

Since Hangman - Challenge does not collect any personal or usage data, we do not store, process, or share any information about you. All game data, which is high score, remains solely on your device, ensuring complete user privacy.

3. Third-Party Services

Google AdMob for Advertising: Hangman - Challenge uses Google AdMob to display rewarded video advertisements. These ads appear only after successfully guessing 4 words correctly. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers for ad serving
  • General device information (device type, operating system)
  • Ad interaction data

We do not use banner ads or interstitial ads. Only video ads are implemented. For more information about Google AdMob's data practices, please visit their Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google Play Store: The app is distributed through Google Play Store, which may collect download and usage statistics according to their privacy policy. We do not have access to this information.

No Other Third-Party Services: Hangman - Challenge does not use any analytics services, subscription management systems, or other third-party tracking tools.

4. Security

Hangman - Challenge prioritizes user privacy and data security. Since the app does not collect or store any personal information on external servers, there is no risk of unauthorized access, disclosure, or data breaches from our side. All game data is securely stored locally on the device using Core Data and is not shared with third parties.

However, since all data is stored locally, uninstalling the app will permanently delete all saved high scores and game progress. We recommend keeping the app installed if you wish to maintain your game records.

5. Children's Privacy

Hangman - Challenge is suitable for children aged 4 and above. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children of any age. Since no personal data is collected from any users, including children, there are no special privacy concerns related to younger users. Parents can feel confident allowing their children to play the game as no personal information is requested or stored.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to our information practices. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy with an updated "Last Update" date. Continued use of the application after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hangmandeveloper@gmail.com.
