Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 8, 2025

About the Application

Hangman is a word guessing game developed as a mobile application. This privacy policy describes how your personal data is collected, used, and protected when using the application.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

  • Game Statistics: High score, current score, and game progress
  • Ad Interaction Data: Advertisement display and interaction information through Google Mobile Ads

Local Storage: All game data is stored locally on your device and is not sent to any servers.

Personally Identifiable Information

Our application does NOT COLLECT the following personal information:

  • Name, surname, or other identity information
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location information
  • Camera or microphone access
  • Contact list

Data Usage

Collected data is used only for the following purposes:

  • Game Experience: Score tracking and saving game progress
  • App Development: Improving game performance
  • Advertisement Display: For rewarded ad functionality

Advertisements and Third-Party Services

Google Mobile Ads

Our application uses Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) to display rewarded advertisements. This service:

  • May use advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID)
  • May collect device information
  • May record ad interaction data

Important: For detailed information about Google's advertising services, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

Ad Personalization

You can disable personalized ads from iOS settings:

  • Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising
  • Turn off "Personalized Ads"

Data Security

Your data security is our priority:

  • Local Storage: All data is securely stored on your device
  • Encryption: Sensitive data is stored in encrypted form
  • Limited Access: Only the application can access necessary data
  • No Data Transfer: Game data is not transferred to any servers

Children's Privacy

Users Under 13: Our application does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, please contact us.

Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When significant changes occur:

  • We will send in-app notifications
  • We will update the "Last Updated" date on this page
  • We will make announcements on our App Store page

User Rights

As a user, you have the following rights:

  • Data Deletion: You can remove all your data by deleting the application
  • Ad Settings: You can change your ad preferences from device settings
  • Information Request: You have the right to know what data is being collected
